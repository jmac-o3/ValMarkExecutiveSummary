Campaign Performance Insights

2025 Performance Highlights

Performance Max Campaign Excellence

The "C - PMax - New Chevy Silverado 1500" campaign generated 11,813 conversions, establishing Performance Max as the cornerstone of lead generation strategy. This validates the investment in automated bidding and broad match targeting approaches.

Service Campaign Efficiency

Service-focused campaigns achieved 33% CTR for branded service campaigns, representing the highest performance across all campaign types. This demonstrates strong brand recognition and effective service marketing positioning.

Inventory Management Insights

New car inventory tracking from May through November reveals inventory levels ranging from 103 units (August, correlating with strong turnover) to 151 units (October-November, building for Q4). August demonstrated optimal efficiency with 103 units supporting 65 sales for 63.1% turnover. September's higher inventory of 129 units with only 52 sales resulted in the lowest turnover at 40.3%, suggesting inventory optimization opportunities.

Seasonal Performance Patterns

Q1 demonstrated peak performance with March leading at 138 sales and 1.05M key events. Summer months showed challenges with September reaching yearly lows (81 sales, 647K key events), indicating opportunities for enhanced warm-weather marketing strategies.
